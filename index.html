Wasted Breath?

A voting booth, a lecture, a physical response.
Do you know what populism is? Neither do we.

Join us on our journey of honest discussion, spontaneous movement; physically discovering what we can do when we’re exhausted with the current system. Trying to find out if there is something we can build with the blocks - to find answers to our questions.

Sparky Sparky BOOM Theatre

Sparky Sparky Boom Theatre (SSBT) is a radically visual postdramatic theatre company.
Our company’s method is to ‘explode’ current and widespread issues.
SSBT utilises choreographed movement to create work which constructs simple but engaging narratives which act to convey the themes of the performance, focusing on “what moves us” to create the meanings of our shows.
