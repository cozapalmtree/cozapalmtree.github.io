Our Company

Sparky Sparky BOOM Theatre was formed in September 2024.

Our core team are James Baker, Kodi Laxton, Coralie Palmer and Katie Seller

Sparky Sparky Boom Theatre (SSBT) is a radically visual postdramatic theatre company.

Our company’s method is to ‘explode’ current and widespread issues. SSBT utilises choreographed movement to create work which constructs simple but engaging narratives which act to convey the themes of the performance, focusing on “what moves us” to create the meanings of our shows.

We aim to tour our performances to rural communities to start conversations about politcs through art
