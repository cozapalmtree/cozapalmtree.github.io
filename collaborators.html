Our Collaborators

Below is a list of people/companies that Sparky are currently or have previously worked with on our projects.

Jonah Marvin - The Archive

Musician

Sparky Sparky BOOM Theatre are currently working with Jonah to develop an original soundtrack from Wasted Breath? that complements the phsyical performance and connects with the show's powerful aesthetic.

Jonah is a Nottinghamshire based music artist who is a recent Confetti graduate. He releases his music under "The Archive"

He produces music for theatre, games, films, animation and TV.

Holly Spiller-Bigley

Performer

Holly is a Co-Founder of SSBT and recent graduate of Drama and Theatre at the University of Lincoln.

Holly performed with us in Wasted Breath? (2025) at the Lincoln Arts Centre.

Holly continues to work with Sparky at a distance as an associate member assisting with dramaturgy, choreography and costume design

Jessica Parry

Performer

Jess is a Co-Founder of SSBT and recent graduate of Drama and Theatre at the University of Lincoln.

Jess performed with us in Wasted Breath? (2025) at the Lincoln Arts Centre.

Jess is currently studying in Devon to become a teacher, we wish her all the best in her future endeavours!
